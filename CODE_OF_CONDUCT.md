# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Showing empathy and respect
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Being collaborative and inclusive

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Disruptive behavior that derails constructive collaboration

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and public interactions representing the project.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior via private security advisory or direct maintainer contact.
